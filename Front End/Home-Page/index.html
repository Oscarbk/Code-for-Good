Leadership Volenteer

Leadership Volenteers serve on one of several committees, help plan events, and are a major part of the activities that the ALS Florida Chapter organizes.

Mission & Care Volenteer

Mission and Care Volenteers help spread awareness and the goals of the ALS Association. They also serve as companions to those living with ALS and help them with daily activities.

Day of Event Volenteer

Enjoy contributing to something bigger? Then join the Day of event Volunteers and be apart of the team that makes things happen. From fundraisers to support groups, they are essential operations of any event that the ALS Association organizes.

Skill Based

Everyone has a special talent, tell us about yours! Together we can find the perfect place for you to use your skills to the fullest potential. Whether your skilled in Web Development or Marketing to anything in between, you could be a huge help to our organization!
